Our Ethos

We are just two lifelong mates from the North East of England who were fed up with the state of the collectible games market. Like many fans, we struggled to find stock of their favourite trading cards and gaming products - constantly beaten to the punch by bots, resellers, and scalpers. What began as frustration turned into action: they created a platform with one clear goal - make collectibles accessible again.

At our core, we’re an affiliate-based company built on fairness, transparency, and a genuine love for the hobby. We keep an eye on stock across multiple platforms so collectors don’t have to. Our mission is straightforward. Help real fans get the products they actually want, without the stress, the FOMO, or inflated prices that take the fun out of collecting. We’re not interested in playing the scalpers’ game. We’re here to beat it.

Community is at the heart of everything we do. We’re collectors ourselves, which means we understand the frustration, the excitement, and the disappointment that comes with the hobby. That’s why we always put people before profit, share real-time restock updates, and aim to make access as fair as possible. At the end of the day, we’re just two marras looking out for the community.
